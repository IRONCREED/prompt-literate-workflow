# Artifact Policy

Generated artifacts are outputs, not source of truth.

## Candidate status

A generated artifact remains a candidate until it has been reviewed, validated against contracts and scenarios, and recorded in TRACE.

## Acceptance boundary

Accepted artifacts must have deterministic evidence: smoke-check output, test report, or an explicit documentation-only status where executable evidence is not applicable.

## Manual edits

Manual edits to generated artifacts must be reflected in the plan, contracts, or TRACE before acceptance. Hidden manual edits break traceability.
