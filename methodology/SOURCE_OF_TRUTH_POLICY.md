# Source of Truth Policy

## Primary source

The human-authored literate plan is the canonical source of truth. It defines intent, chunk structure, constraints, and the readable explanation of the work.

## Supporting sources

`CONTRACTS.md`, `SCENARIOS.md`, prompts, tests, and TRACE support the plan. They do not replace it.

## Candidate inputs

External authoring or review surfaces are candidate inputs until they are normalized, validated, reviewed, and committed into the canonical source tree.

## Generated outputs

Generated artifacts are outputs. They are never source of truth. They may be accepted only after contract review, deterministic validation, and TRACE recording.
